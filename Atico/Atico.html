Ático

Between the illuminated rooftops of Gran Vía with the frenetic rhythm of Madrid as a backdrop there is a place where the most creative and original flavours are combined with masterly technique and carefully selected products. An elegant ambience, sophisticated but familiar that invites a journey through our culinary heritage by referencing different corners of Spain through its varied dishes.

Ramón Freixa, two Michelin Stars, leads a team that makes Ático an unforgettable gastronomic experience Whimsy and common sense are balanced perfectly; it’s what defines each plate by Ramón Freixa served here. A cuisine that will be appreciated by everybody and that has won well deserved recognition. At Ático the roots of Freixa’s culinary background can be discovered through the tastes of the Mediterranean and its unmistakable daring, innovation and sensibility.

Location: Madrid center

Dining room capacities: 70 pax

Price Level: average-high

Style: Author-modern cuisine

Vegetarian Options/Special diets: Available
